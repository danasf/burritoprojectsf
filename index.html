Who We Are

We're a community supported group that gets together on the last Monday of each month to make and deliver burritos to the community living on the street.

Where & When

Location

Martin De Porres House Foundation
225 Potrero Avenue
San Francisco, CA 94103

Event Details

  • 4:00 pm to 5:00 pm: preparing Rice, Beans, and Salsa (all animal free) and setting up assembly line.
  • 5:00 pm to 5:45: Building and Rolling out burritos
  • 5:45 pm to 7:00 pm: Distributing Burritos

Make sure to wear comfortable shoes and if you plan delivering burritos bring a backpack that can hold up to 20 burritos. Thank you for your support.
